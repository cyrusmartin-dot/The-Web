How a Device Connects to the Internet

A visual and simple guide to how your device goes online.

Step 1: Your Device

Your journey starts with a device like a smartphone, tablet, or computer.

Step 2: Home Router / Wi-Fi

Your device connects to a home router through Wi-Fi or a wired connection.

Step 3: Internet Service Provider (ISP)

The router forwards your request to your Internet Service Provider (ISP).

Step 4: Domain Name System (DNS)

DNS translates the website name (like google.com) into an IP address.
